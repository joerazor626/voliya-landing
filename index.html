Features

Everything You Need to Grow

From creating promotions to tracking engagement — Voliya gives businesses the tools to succeed in Fiji's digital economy.

Promotions & Deals

Create percentage-off, BOGO, and fixed-amount deals. Schedule them, set limits, and track redemptions in real time.

Geofencing

Set location boundaries around your stores. Customers get push notifications when they're nearby — driving real foot traffic.

Digital Flyers & Campaigns

Publish weekly flyers, catalogs, and event promotions. Track views, clicks, and shares — all from one dashboard.

Analytics Dashboard

See how your promotions perform. Track engagement, redemptions, and customer reach with beautiful, real-time charts.

Gamification & Rewards

Earn badges, climb tiers from Bronze to Platinum, and track streaks. Engaged businesses get more visibility on the platform.

Multi-Location Support

Manage all your branches from one account. Set unique geofences and promotions per location — perfect for franchises.
